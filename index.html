About Me

Hello there! My name is Aashrith Beesabathuni, a student at UC Berkeley studying EECS (Electrical Engineering + Computer Science) and Mechanical Engineering. My main area of interest is exploring the blend of hardware and software to create robotic systems to do anything and everything in between. The only way to learn is by "doing" so I love to get hands on experience to learn new skills! On the non-technical end, I love hiking around the bay and spending time outdoors. I recently bought an acoustic guitar and am learning some of my favorite songs on there!
