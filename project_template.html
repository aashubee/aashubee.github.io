Project Overview

As a member of CRB (Combat Robotics at Berkeley), me and my team designed and built a 15 pound combat robot for the Sacremento Bot Battles. With a ___ wheel drive and an egg beater style weapon, HOLY CR@B made it to the semi-finals of the compettiion, with an impressive K.O count!

Design Overview

As all good projects do, my team started with brainstorming and ideation of what we wanted our robot to look like. I led a chunk of this process, allowing us to settle on ideas earlier on that other teams. We priotized speed in our robot, s utilzing a 4WD with 2 motors was the best option. We also used an eggebater style weapon donated to us by a previous bot, allowing us to save costs and invest in other important areas of the bot. The bot was designed in Fusion 360, which was a relatively new platform for me. Using my previous experinece in Autodesk Inventor and Solidwokrs, I was able to complete a large portion of the design, focusign on the drive systems and the weapon structure.

Outcome and Achievements

At the Sacremento Bot Battles 2023, HOLY CR@B !! went on to win second place at the comptetion, loosing the last match due to a bad battery connection. I drove the bot for the entire competition, which was a very fun and engaging experince. We had some amazing fights, and more importantly I was able to learn so much from all the capable builders at the competion, from college students to very experienced engineers.
